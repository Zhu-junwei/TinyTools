FixWin 11 for Windows 11 and Windows 10

We are pleased to release FixWin 11 for Windows 11 and Windows 10. It is a freeware portable application to repair & fix common Windows 11/10 annoyances & issues. It does not require to be installed.

We first suggest that you to create a System Restore Point.

Having done that, apply at most one fix at a time and restart your computer.
